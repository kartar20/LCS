    Growing together
    Supporting each other

    Head's Welcome

    A warm welcome to Littleover Community School,

    Our aims are simple, but they underpin everything we expect from our students, and they underpin the vision for our school.

    In our school,  students develop a love of lifelong learning, understanding that developing their knowledge and skills empowers them. Our engaging and diverse curriculum allows students to flourish.

    In our school, students contribute to the caring and supportive environment, knowing that compassion and thoughtfulness are characteristics which are celebrated and should be demonstrated in all their thoughts and actions.

    In our school, students succeed, both through academic excellence and through achievements beyond the traditional curriculum, valuing the resilience and effort required to achieve the highest standards for the community and individually.

    As a result, our expectations are high. Staff, students and parents  work together to ensure that all members of our community feel valued.

    We look forward to welcoming you into the community.

    Virtual Tour

    First experiences are easier to navigate if you know what to expect. Take our virtual interactive tour for a behind-the-scenes look at our facilities – a welcoming, safe and supportive environment where your child can thrive.

    Dates & Events

    Never miss a thing, and keep your child’s learning on track by checking term dates, upcoming trips, and events. There’s so much to look forward to during the school year at LCS!

    Extracurricular

    Part of the fun of studying at LCS is taking learning beyond the classroom. Our students can expand their existing skills and explore new ones in various extracurricular activities before and after school.

    Putting Learning First

    At LCS, learning always comes first. Our core purpose is education, and we’re committed to meeting the needs of our students with a broad, deep, balanced educational programme.

    Caring for our students, and each other.

    We’re strongly committed to safeguarding and keeping each other safe and healthy. We’re proud of our relationship with our students and consistently work towards an inclusive school culture.

    Learning

    Achieving academically

    Lifelong learners never stop growing, often exceeding their ambitions and potential educationally and vocationally. Our expert staff deliver a high-quality, inclusive academic programme that encourages our students to be and stay curious in the classroom and beyond. 

    Our school day

    Caring, Fun, and Structured Days at LCS

    A typical LCS day consists of five periods, tutor time, morning break and lunchtime. We work to a ‘Week A’ and ‘Week B’ timetable, starting with Week A on your child’s first morning at school. Once they’ve started, you can find more information on timetables, homework, and other information at Go4Schools.
