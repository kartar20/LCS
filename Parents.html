    Key Information

    Information for parents and carers

    Whether your child is just starting school or an LCS student, they’ll thrive with your support. You’ll find everything you need on this page to help them navigate the school year successfully.

    If you are the carer or parent of a Sixth Form student then please look at the information under the Sixth Form menu in the first instance.

    Induction Information

    Our Induction Programme is designed to help students feel confident and well-prepared for the transition to secondary school, with opportunities to meet key staff, get to know their form group, and experience a range of activities that reflect life at LCS.

    General Information

    Essential information including getting to school, rules and conduct, and letters / forms relating to a range of topics from absence requests through to parents' evenings.

    Dates & Events

    Never miss a thing, and keep your child’s learning on track by checking term dates, upcoming trips, and events. There’s so much to look forward to during the school year at LCS!

    Exams

    Get ready for success by accessing all the information you need to support your child through preparation for exams, results day and beyond. There’s no time like the present – you’ve got this.

    Extracurricular

    Part of the fun of studying at LCS is taking learning beyond the classroom. Our students can expand their existing skills and explore new ones in various extracurricular activities before and after school.

    Work Experience

    Discover how Year 11 students at Littleover Community School gain valuable experience through a one-week work placement. Find key dates, resources, and support to help your child prepare.

    Admissions

    Navigating admissions

    At LCS, we’re proud of our diverse student community. On applying, your child takes the first step in preparing themselves to progress confidently in the vibrant world we live in. We welcome all applications. Please join us for an open event or read about the local authority admissions process. Don’t hesitate to get in touch if you have questions.

I really enjoy studying at Uni and the skills I gained at Littleover have made the transition easier, specifically negotiating physics cake days in lessons has helped me befriend flatmates with my improved baking skills. A levels are hard, but survivable with the right help.

    I started my time with LCS sixth form off the back of two years of GCSE’s, which were massively interrupted by the Covid pandemic. LCS did everything in its power to give me the best possible opportunities to succeed, despite these setbacks. Throughout A-levels, the online portal, Go4schools made managing homework and viewing grades very straightforward, and meant that all the information I needed to study was easily accessible. LCS Sixth form provided me with all the support I needed to complete my A-levels to a high standard. This included good textbooks and dedicated, approachable, enthusiastic teachers, as well as good facilities and opportunities for trips and competitions.

LCS Sixth Form has also supported me throughout the University application process, providing invaluable guidance and feedback, enabling me to achieve my goals.
